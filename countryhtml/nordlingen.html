EXPLORE NÖRDLINGEN

The real-world inspiration for Attack on Titan's walled city. Discover a perfectly preserved medieval town inside a meteor crater, with its intact city walls that inspired the iconic anime series.

Why Visit Nördlingen

Discover what makes Nördlingen a fascinating destination for history buffs and anime fans alike

Bavarian Cuisine

Enjoy authentic Bavarian specialties and local delicacies in historic restaurants and beer gardens within the walled city.

Attack on Titan Connection

Walk the circular walled city that directly inspired the setting for the popular anime Attack on Titan (Shingeki no Kyojin).

Meteor Crater

The only town in the world built entirely within a meteor impact crater, with buildings containing rare suevite rock fragments.

Medieval Time Capsule

One of Germany's best-preserved medieval towns with half-timbered buildings, cobblestone streets, and a complete 14th-century wall.

Top Attractions

Must-visit places that capture the essence of Nördlingen

Complete City Wall

Walk the intact 2.7km circular wall reminiscent of the walls in Attack on Titan that protected humanity

St. George's Church Tower

Climb the "Daniel" tower for panoramic views similar to the Scout Regiment's vantage points

Rieskrater Museum

Learn about the meteor impact that formed the crater 15 million years ago

Market Square

The central gathering place reminiscent of scenes from the anime's inner districts

Attack on Titan Tours

Specialized tours highlighting connections between the medieval town and the popular anime

Baldinger Gate

One of the five medieval gates resembling the gates that open in the anime's walls

Nördlingen Gallery

Glimpses of the medieval walled city that inspired Attack on Titan
