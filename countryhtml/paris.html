Why Visit Paris

Discover what makes Paris one of the world's most fascinating cities to explore

Culinary Paradise

Home to some of the world's finest restaurants and pastry shops, Paris offers extraordinary dining experiences from charming cafés to Michelin-starred establishments.

Rich Heritage

Discover historic monuments, museums, and gardens that showcase France's cultural heritage and create magnificent landmarks throughout the City of Light.

Art & Culture

Experience world-class museums, galleries and cultural institutions that have made Paris the global capital of art and intellectual thought for centuries.

Shopping Paradise

From luxury boutiques in Avenue Montaigne to vintage finds in Le Marais, Paris is a shopper's dream with endless fashion and retail experiences.

Top Attractions

Must-visit places that capture the essence of Paris

Eiffel Tower

Paris's iconic iron lattice tower offering spectacular views of the city

Louvre Museum

The world's largest art museum housing masterpieces like the Mona Lisa

Notre-Dame Cathedral

A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture and one of Paris's most visited monuments

Montmartre

A historic hilltop district known for its artistic history and Sacré-Cœur Basilica

Arc de Triomphe

A monumental arch honoring those who fought for France, offering panoramic views

Champs-Élysées

Paris's most famous avenue lined with luxury shops, cafés, and theaters

Paris Gallery

Glimpses of life in France's elegant capital
