Why Visit Tokyo

Discover what makes Tokyo one of the world's most fascinating cities to explore

Culinary Paradise

Home to more Michelin-starred restaurants than any other city in the world, Tokyo offers extraordinary dining experiences from street food to fine dining.

Rich Heritage

Discover ancient temples, shrines, and gardens that showcase Japan's cultural heritage and create peaceful sanctuaries within the bustling city.

Technology Hub

Experience cutting-edge technology and innovations that offer a glimpse into the future, from robot restaurants to electronic districts.

Shopping Paradise

From luxury boutiques in Ginza to vintage finds in Shimokitazawa, Tokyo is a shopper's dream with endless retail experiences.

Top Attractions

Must-visit places that capture the essence of Tokyo

Shinjuku

Tokyo's bustling business and entertainment district with the world's busiest railway station

Meiji Shrine

A serene Shinto shrine set in a beautiful forest in the heart of Tokyo

Shibuya Crossing

The world's busiest pedestrian crossing, an iconic symbol of modern Tokyo

Senso-ji Temple

Tokyo's oldest temple in historic Asakusa, with vibrant shopping streets

Tokyo Skytree

The tallest tower in Japan offering breathtaking panoramic views

Harajuku

The epicenter of Japanese youth culture and fashion trends

Tokyo Gallery

Glimpses of life in Japan's dynamic capital
